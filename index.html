LLP Registration

Why Choose LLP Registration?

A Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) is distinct from partnership firms, with the liability and duties of partners limited to their commitments. It is cost-effective to establish, requires no mandatory audit for medium and independent companies, and involves minimal guidelines and agreements. LLPs do not have a minimum capital requirement, and responsibilities can be easily transferred by appointing a new Designated Partner. With perpetual succession, an LLP endures until dissolved by mutual agreement among its partners.
